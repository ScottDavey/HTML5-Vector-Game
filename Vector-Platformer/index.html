The purpose of this demo was to test out a more organic form of collision detection using lines instead of the common tile-based approach. As the player moves across the screen, notice that he's following the dips and bumps of the terrain. To see a more visual representation of what the collision lines look like, follow this link to the Vector Map editor: https://scottdavey.github.io/HTML5-Vector-Game/Vector-Mapper

Controls:
  • Movement: W, A, S, D OR Keyboard Arrows
  • Jump: SPACE
  • Return to Menu: ESCAPE
